# Hey, I'm Lionel 👾

I'm a Vancouver-based creative technology artist utilizing code as a paintbrush to tease curiosity and provoke critical self-reflection. Drawing from a background in software engineering, my work bridges the gap between digital algorithms and the physical world, using machines and hardware to demystify the technology that surrounds us.

I work under [Ucodia Studio](https://ucodia.space) and as a research assistant at [SFU Metacreation Lab](https://metacreation.net), where I help maintain [**Autolume**](https://github.com/Metacreation-Lab/autolume), a no-code generative AI system for artists to train, craft, and perform with their own models in real time.

## What I'm working on

🎨 **[Autolume](https://github.com/Metacreation-Lab/autolume)** · Core maintainer. Autolume lets artists use StyleGAN2 for live visual performances with real-time latent space exploration, OSC control, and NDI output. I work across the full stack with a focus on user experience and developer experience.

⚡ **[Wattsup](https://wattsup.tech)** · Founder. A sustainability project shining a light on AI energy usage. Exhibited at MUTEK Montreal 2025 as part of the AI Ecologies Lab. Try [Wattsup for OpenRouter](https://openrouter.wattsup.tech) for a live window into global AI energy consumption.

🍄 **[Mycologue](https://mycologue.org)** · A 3D catalogue of mushrooms.

🧰 **[dtst](https://github.com/ucodia/dtst)** · An image dataset toolkit for ML training pipelines.

## Selected projects

⏳ **[flowtime](https://github.com/ucodia/flowtime)** · An art project and thought experiment on flow states. Uses seeded PRNGs to compute a deterministic but unpredictable alternative time. Available as a JS library, a CLI, and a [Casio watch firmware](https://github.com/ucodia/flowtime-watch).

✏️ **[u5.js](https://github.com/ucodia/u5.js)** · A minimalist canvas animation library. Lightweight alternative to p5.js for when you want structure without the weight.

📷 **[threedify](https://github.com/ucodia/threedify)** · CLI tool for photogrammetry on macOS. Turn a folder of photos into a 3D model. Installable via Homebrew.

☄️ **[infinite-chaos](https://github.com/ucodia/infinite-chaos)** · Generative chaotic attractors rendered as high-resolution images.

## Background

10+ years as a professional software developer (ex-Amazon), now focused full-time on creative technology. My work has been exhibited at [MUTEK](https://forum.mutek.org/en/speakers/lionel-ringenbach) and featured through the Metacreation Lab. I run workshops teaching artists to work with generative AI tools.

I care about lightweight tools, self-hosted infrastructure, and building software that respects the people using it.
